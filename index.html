ABOUT ME

My name is Chuck Obaze, I am a graphic and web designer currently working and living in Atlanta, GA. I graduated with a Bachelors of Fine Arts degree in Graphic and Web Design and i have a passion for clean and simple designs. I love writing code, i fell in love with it after taking a class in HTML as an elective while college and ever since, my focus has been not only designing websites but making them function dynamically. Besides design, i love doing photography, videography, watching soccer (BIG TOTTENHAM HOTSPUR F.C. FAN), attending concerts, and enjoying a cold draft Belgian white with friends.

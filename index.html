MY PROCESS

RESEARCH
Research is the first and most crucial step in my design process. This enables me gather ideas, and further understand the project.

SKETCHES
My ideas come to life in this stage. After gathering ideas, i explore the initial look and feel by sketching several possibilities.

MOCK UP
A digital mockup brings the a couple sketches of my choice to life. This step allows for critiquing, changes and tweaks of ideas digitally.

DESIGN
At this point, i already have the structure down. I proceed to add color, typography, photographs and so on before final delivery to client.

MY PHILOSOPHY

I hold strong belief in the use of design for the good of humanity, either by making websites more accessible and easy to use, or by designing or redesigning products that can enhance the quality of life.
